Workflow

How it works

Four steps. Nothing to configure beyond your normal review habits.

  1. 01

    Describe your circuit

    Write what you need—blocks, interfaces, constraints. Plain language is fine.

  2. 02

    ProtoFlow generates a schematic

    First-pass symbols, nets, and sheets on disk. Expect to edit.

  3. 03

    Refine if needed

    Fix parts, run cleanup (Ctrl-U), check against the datasheet.

  4. 04

    Export to KiCad or Altium

    Hand off for layout, DRC, and release in the tool you already use.

ProtoFlow is not a PCB editor. It does not replace your layout toolchain.
