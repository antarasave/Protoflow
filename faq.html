FAQ

Direct answers — short, engineer-to-engineer. Update any product-specific limits as you ship.

Does ProtoFlow replace KiCad or Altium?

No. It targets schematic capture and prep, then export. Layout, DRC, manufacturing outputs, and org-wide library governance stay in your EDA toolchain.

Will the AI get my design “right” on the first try?

Assume no. Treat AI output as a first draft. You are responsible for datasheet-accurate pinouts, ratings, and design review—same as any generated code.

Where do my files live?

On your machine for the desktop workflow. If you add cloud features later, say exactly what leaves the device and when.

Do I need a credit card to download?

No for this landing page’s promise—swap in your real billing policy if that changes.

What should I verify after export?

Symbol pin maps, power trees, default footprints, ERC in your EDA tool, and any analog-sensitive nets. Export is a handoff, not sign-off.
